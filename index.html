Biography

Linus tovalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the crreator and principal developer of the Linux kernel,which is the kernel for a number of operating system.He also created the version control system Git.Torvalds was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki,Finland.He studied comptur science at the University of Helsinki,and he developed the Linux kernel as a student project in 1991.since then ,the Linux kernel has become one of the most widely kernels in the world,powering everything from smartphones to supercompters.

  • 2010-Received the 2010 Millenium Technology prize and the 2012 Free software Foundation Award for the Adavancement of Free software
  • 2013-Inducted into the Internet Hall of fame in 2013 for his contribution of the development of the Linux kernel and the creation of Git
